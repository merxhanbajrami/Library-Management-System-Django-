Our mission

Our mission is to inspire meaningful connections across the world through reading and discussion. Our vision for Books&Beyond is grounded in a deep appreciation for the positive transformation that takes place in every person who is passionate about reading.

Except discussing and meetings that you get, you will also be able to borrow books from our stock, which include a lot of categories, such as: Philosophy, Psychology, Science, Novels, Astronomy etc.
